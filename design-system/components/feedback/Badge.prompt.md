A small label for categories, statuses and certifications.

```jsx
<Badge tone="blue">Innovations</Badge>
<Badge tone="accent" size="sm">ISO 13485</Badge>
<Badge tone="on-dark">Live</Badge>   {/* on navy/photo */}
```

Tones: `blue` (default), `navy`, `accent` (division crimson), `neutral`, `on-dark`. Sizes `sm` / `md`. Square 4px corners to match buttons.
